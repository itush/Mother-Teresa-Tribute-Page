Mother Teresa
1910-1997



If you can't feed a hundred people, then feed just one.

Life of Mother Teresa

  • 1910-19 : Born on 26 August 1910 in Skopje. She was the youngest child of Nikollë and Dranafile Bojaxhiu (Bernai). At the age of eight she lost her father.
  • 1929 : Arrived in India.
  • 1931 : Took 1st religious vows.
  • 1950 : Founded the Missionaries of Charity.
  • 1981 : Founded the Corpus Christi Movement for Priests.
  • 1983 : 1st heart attack.
  • 1989 : 2nd heart attack.
  • 1996 : Broke collarbone.
  • 1997 : Resigned as head of the Missionaries of Charity on 13 March , and died on 5 September.
